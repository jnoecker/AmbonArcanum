# Race Descriptions — Recovered from Asset Manifest

## HUMAN (Archae)
The Archae are the ancestral human race of Ambon — lithe of build with warm bronze skin, elongated earlobes, and subtle musculature. Of average height, they carry themselves with quiet dignity and an ancient heritage. They favor flowing robes woven with moss green and soft gold threads that speak to their deep connection with the natural world.

## ELF (Lustriae)
The Lustriae are the elven folk of the Fae Wood — eternally youthful with delicate features, silver-touched hair, and diaphanous ornate wings that unfurl like woven time itself. They dress in flowing garments of dusty rose and moss green shimmering with soft gold thread. Gifted with chronomancy, flight, and foresight, they radiate a playful curiosity tempered by approachable dignity. Time sprites drift in their wake.

## HALFLING
Halflings stand about half the height of humans, with round friendly faces, warm expressions, and slightly pointed ears. Their comfortable, well-fed builds speak to a love of good food and their burrow-dwelling heritage. They wear cozy earth-toned clothing with copper buttons and soft fabrics, often with a luck charm or small trinket at their belt. Tiny wildflowers and clover seem to bloom in their footsteps — a sign of their supernatural luck. Despite their comfortable appearance, they possess boundless curiosity and surprising courage.

## MEDUSAE (Jellykin)
The Medusae are ocean-dwelling jellyfolk with translucent, jellyfish-like skin that shifts between soft bioluminescent hues of lavender, pale blue, and dusty rose, reflecting their current mood. Their humanoid forms are gracefully elongated with flowing jellyfish bell-shaped head features and long fluorescent hair tendrils that cascade like living ribbons, pulsing with tiny glowing barbs of moss green and soft gold light. They wear garments woven from manipulated coral fibers and dwell in sophisticated underwater cities of magically-shaped, glowing coral structures.

## AETHERAE
The Aetherae manifest as flowing silhouettes of living shadow — misty, semi-corporeal beings with glowing cerulean eyes that pierce through fog. They drape themselves in layered cloaks of deep charcoal and soft gold, embroidered with moss green patterns. Possessed of telekinetic abilities, they can be seen with light threads weaving through the fog around their outstretched hands. They exude quiet magic and approachable dignity, their forms blurring at the edges into atmospheric haze.

## ALORAE
The Alorae are celestial beings of prismatic light, their most striking feature being stained-glass wings that refract light in brilliant rainbows across their backs. A shifting aurora-like halo of radiance encircles their heads. They wear culturally resonant attire woven from iridescent threads that shimmer with moss green and dusty rose undertones. An ambient glow emanates from their very core, diffusing through any scene they inhabit. They embody celestial grace and approachable wisdom.

## MYCORAE
The Mycorae are fungal humanoids with velvety indigo skin and cascading lion's manes of soft gold fungal tendrils that glow faintly with moss green bioluminescence. Their attire is a flowing, organic tapestry of dusty rose and pale blue mycelial fibers, woven with living vines and embedded with luminescent spore clusters. They dwell among towering fungal trees with lavender-hued caps, connected to vast underground mycelium networks through which they share communal wisdom. They embody tranquil mystery and collective consciousness.
